OUTPUT CENTER
Package, encrypt and return outputs.

Output Center handles final delivery after production and QC. Results can be downloaded or returned to user-designated storage. Encryption is selected here as a delivery option.

Output package may include:
Report, KML, GeoJSON, CSV, raster package, tile package, QC summary, source note and delivery log.
