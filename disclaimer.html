4. TLE Prediction and Orbit-related Outputs

TLE pass prediction outputs are planning and preliminary assessment products. They are generated from the provided orbit element sets and selected calculation settings. TLE-based results may vary with TLE freshness, satellite maneuvers, atmospheric drag, model assumptions, observer coordinate precision and time settings.

The report shall include TLE source, TLE lines, observer location, prediction period, calculation model, map layer source, pass table, ground track or visible segment vectors and limitation notes.

TLE prediction output is not a certified real-time precise orbit determination product and should not be used as the sole basis for mission-critical, safety-critical, regulatory, aviation or emergency decisions without independent validation.
