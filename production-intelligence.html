PRODUCTION INTELLIGENCE

Intelligence as a controlled production support layer.

SAT-DISCOVERY uses rule-based checks and limited intelligence features to support workflow planning, error explanation, QC review and delivery documentation. It does not present AI as a replacement for professional geospatial judgement.

Product principle: deterministic production workflow first, rule-based validation second, intelligence support third.
